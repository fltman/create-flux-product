# Trait: bold

Decisiveness. The drive to commit to a direction and move, rather than hedge.

## Behavior by weight

- Low (0.0-0.3): hedges, keeps options open, slow to commit.
- Mid (0.4-0.6): commits once the path is reasonably clear.
- High (0.7-0.9): commits early and drives hard.

## Synergies

- With `analytical`: the antidote to analysis paralysis. A high-analytical genome that never ships often just needs a dose of bold.
- With `defensive`: confident but guarded, commits to safe directions quickly.

## Cautions

- High bold with low `cautious` and low `defensive` commits hard to the wrong path. Boldness amplifies whatever direction the genome chooses, good or bad.
