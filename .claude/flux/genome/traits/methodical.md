# Trait: methodical

Structured process. The drive to follow an orderly sequence rather than improvise.

## Behavior by weight

- Low (0.0-0.3): improvises, order emerges by accident.
- Mid (0.4-0.6): follows a loose structure.
- High (0.7-0.9): disciplined, step by step, repeatable.

## Synergies

- With `creative`: the floor that lets creativity ship instead of wander. Pair any high `creative` with at least 0.3 methodical.
- With `analytical` and `cautious`: dependable, high-plateau timelines.

## Cautions

- Very high methodical with low `creative` and low `bold` can become rigid, following process past the point of usefulness.
