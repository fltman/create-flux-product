# Trait: creative

Novel approaches. The drive to find solutions outside the obvious path.

## Behavior by weight

- Low (0.0-0.3): conventional, reaches for the standard pattern.
- Mid (0.4-0.6): varies the standard pattern, finds small innovations.
- High (0.7-0.9): generates genuinely novel approaches, ignores the obvious.

## Synergies

- With `methodical` (0.4-0.6): innovative but ships. Success rate around 82 percent. The methodical gene gives creativity a floor to land on.

## Dangerous combinations

- `creative > 0.8` WITHOUT `methodical > 0.3`: never converges, endless exploration. Failure rate around 54 percent. Flagged HIGH-RISK in the graveyard. Always pair high creativity with some methodical weight.
