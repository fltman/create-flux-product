# Trait: cautious

Risk awareness. The drive to weigh consequences and avoid irreversible mistakes before committing.

## Behavior by weight

- Low (0.0-0.3): commits quickly, accepts risk.
- Mid (0.4-0.6): checks the main risks, then proceeds.
- High (0.7-0.9): weighs every path, prefers reversible and safe moves.

## Synergies

- With `methodical` and a domain skill: steady, dependable timelines that plateau high. Strong for compliance, finance, and healthcare contexts.

## Cautions

- High cautious with low `bold` and low `aggressive` produces safe but slow timelines that may lose on efficiency. Good for security-critical work, a handicap for prototyping.
